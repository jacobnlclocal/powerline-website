Tree & Shrub Care in Woodruff, SC

Healthy trees and shrubs frame your property and add lasting value. Woodruff properties feature a wide variety of ornamental trees and native species common to Spartanburg County. Our crews are familiar with the species found throughout the area and apply proper care techniques for each.

We time our pruning to match each species' natural growth cycle, ensuring your ornamental plantings bloom their best and maintain an attractive shape year-round.
