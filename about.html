About Powerline Maintenance

Trusted lawn care professionals serving the Upstate South Carolina community since 2010.

Since 2010, Powerline Maintenance has been providing exceptional lawn care and landscaping services to residential and commercial clients across the Clemson, Anderson, and Easley area. What started as a small, neighborhood-focused operation has grown into one of the most trusted lawn maintenance companies in Upstate South Carolina.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to transforming and maintaining beautiful outdoor spaces that enhance your property's value and curb appeal. We combine horticultural expertise with customer-focused service to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and sustainability sets us apart in the lawn maintenance industry. We use eco-friendly products whenever possible and invest in modern equipment to ensure efficient, high-quality results for every property we service.

Why Choose Us?

  • Licensed and insured professionals — Fully covered so you have peace of mind.
  • Eco-friendly lawn care practices — We prioritize products and methods that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment.
  • Custom maintenance plans — Every property is unique. We tailor our services to match your specific needs and budget.
  • Modern equipment and techniques — We invest in the latest tools and stay up-to-date on best practices in the industry.
  • Consistent, reliable service — We show up on time, every time, and deliver the same quality on every visit.
  • Free consultations and estimates — No pressure, no obligation. We'll assess your property and provide an honest quote.
