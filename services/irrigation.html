Irrigation System Services

Smart, efficient irrigation solutions that deliver the right amount of water to your lawn and landscape — protecting your property and reducing waste.

Watering your lawn consistently and correctly is one of the most impactful things you can do for its long-term health. Too little water during the heat of a South Carolina summer leads to dormancy, root stress, and turf loss. Too much water promotes shallow root growth, fungal disease, and wasted resources. A properly designed and maintained irrigation system takes the guesswork out of watering entirely. Powerline Maintenance has been installing, repairing, and servicing irrigation systems for homeowners and businesses across Clemson, Anderson, and Easley, SC since 2010.

Our irrigation work begins with a thorough assessment of your property — accounting for lawn size, plant zones, soil type, slope, and sun exposure — to design a system that applies water evenly and efficiently. We install systems using commercial-grade components, positioned and programmed to minimize overlap, runoff, and dry spots. The result is a lawn and landscape that receives consistent, targeted moisture without the daily hassle of manual watering.

For properties with existing systems, our repair and maintenance services address everything from broken heads and clogged nozzles to controller failures and underground line leaks. Many irrigation problems develop gradually, going unnoticed until water bills spike or sections of the lawn begin to decline. Powerline Maintenance offers both reactive repairs and proactive seasonal maintenance to keep your system performing at its best through the full irrigation season.

What's Included

  • New irrigation system design and installation for residential and commercial properties
  • Zone mapping and head placement for even, efficient water distribution
  • Smart controller programming including weather-based and soil moisture-based scheduling
  • Inspection and diagnosis of existing system performance issues
  • Sprinkler head repair and replacement
  • Underground line leak detection and repair
  • Backflow preventer testing and servicing
  • Spring system startup, including pressure checks and zone-by-zone inspection
  • Fall system winterization to prevent freeze damage

Why a Well-Maintained Irrigation System Matters

An irrigation system that operates at peak efficiency does more than just save water — it directly supports the health of your entire landscape. Consistent, zone-appropriate watering keeps turf roots deep and active, reduces disease pressure from inconsistent wetting and drying cycles, and ensures that ornamental beds and newly established plants receive the moisture they need without over-saturating adjacent areas. Neglected systems with broken heads, misaligned nozzles, or outdated controllers often waste thousands of gallons per season while still failing to deliver adequate coverage. Powerline Maintenance keeps your irrigation system in top condition so your investment in your lawn and landscape is never compromised.
